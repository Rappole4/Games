Safe Passage

Instructions

A quick explanation of this game. The goal is to not only reach the pot of gold inside the maze, but also to have a complete path connecting the gold to the exit. As you click boxes inside the grid your number of moves will go down. If you reach zero moves before you reach the goal, the game will alert you that you have lost and will then reset the game. Answer the questions below to get either hints or directions on how to move through the maze. Answer carefully though, you only get one chance per question and then it is gone. Answers are all one word or number and must be either numberic or spelled correctly.
